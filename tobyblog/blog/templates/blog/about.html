WHAT IS STEAMAGINE?


This website is a full-stack project website for career prospects and a personal project to add to my portfolio. Using the Django framework, I have created this twitter style website. Users can create an account and then create a post for everyone else to see. Users can edit the post as many times as they wish. Users can also then delete their posts that they have made and make changes to their account if they wish to do so in their profile section. Any reason you may wish your information to be deleted and your account removed, please contact me at the email provided.

This website is a personal project for everyone to use. I only ask you use the website as much as you want. Feel free to plug your business or band onto the site for others to see.
